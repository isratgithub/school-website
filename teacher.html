Our Teacher

Teachers are best known for the role of educating the students that are placed in their care. Beyond that, teachers serve many other roles in the classroom. Teachers set the tone of their classrooms, build a warm environment, mentor and nurture students, become role models, and listen and look for signs of trouble.A Teacher is responsible for preparing lesson plans and educating students at all levels. Their duties include assigning homework, grading tests, and documenting progress.
Teachers must be able to instruct in a variety of subjects and reach students with engaging lesson plans.tion to parents, create tests, create and reinforce classroom rules, work with school administration prepare students for standardized tests, and manage.A good teacher is not that hard to find, but you must know where to look. The good teachers are well-prepared in advance for their education goals. They prepare their plan of action every day to ensure maximum productivity. Teachers have a lot of knowledge about everything, specifically in the subject they specialize in. A good teacher expands their knowledge continues to provide good answers to their students.
Similarly, a good teacher is like a friend that helps us in all our troubles.

Our Courses

Muslim Modern Academy is an well known Secondary School situated at Dhaka. It established in 1963. One of the best School in Bangladesh. Muslim Modern Academy Is A Bengali Medium School. It is the oldest high school in this Area. Since 1963, it have been providing better education in Dhaka area. Here our school there are many courses. Student can earn easily by their helpful and co-operative teacher. In the Science section the courses are Physics, Chemestry, Biology, Higher Math.In the Commerce section the courses are Economics, Accounting, Finance and the Arts section the courses are History, Geography, Political science.
